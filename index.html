WHERE
JAIPUR

WHY SHOULD ONE ATTEND
MSS 2024, Jaipur, India

It offers curated and segregated content with separate halls for Beginners and Advanced delegates. In depth total 3 workshops covering Musculoskeletal Ultrasound, Musculoskeletal MRI and Musculoskeletal Interventions along with focus on clinical musculoskeletal Radiology, enabling a radiologist to develop clinical skills and enhance their clinical practice in Musculoskeletal Radiology.  It’ll be a comprehensive 3-day truly world class event covering every practical, theoretical and day to day difficulties faced by a Radiologist in their journey of Musculoskeletal Radiology.  Only case based interactive panel discussions with no didactic lectures. Life changing sessions by experts sharing their life journeys and lessons learnt in field of Musculoskeletal Radiology over decades. Whether you are a resident or a senior consultant, there will be lot to learn and take back from this event for all attendees. 

Clinical Musculoskeletal Ultrasound workshop

Unique workshop with interactive teaching on clinically approaching each Musculoskeletal pathology before doing an ultrasound. This workshop will cover interactive discussions between faculty and delegates on Anatomy of each joint, clinical examination of each pathology and how to do clinically relevant musculoskeletal Ultrasound and POCUS. Hands on musculoskeletal Ultrasound Session will be divided into beginners and advanced groups with separate faculty and different hands-on teaching content on the stations.

MSK MRI
Workshop

This will be a unique dicom cases based interactive workshop where all the delegatess will be given time to look at each DICOM case , followed by in depth discussion by expert faculties on each MRI case. The MRI cases will cover sports Injuries, inflammatory arthritis , Internal derenagement of Joints and all possible spectrum on MSK MRI. Kindly note : The delegates are requested to bring their own laptops for the workshop.

Clinical Musculoskeletal Interventions Workshop
Clinical Musculoskeletal Interventions Hands on workshop 9:1 Delegates: Faculty/Cadaver Ratio Hands on workshop will cover both Ultrasound guided and C arm guided MSK Interventions in sports medicine, pain management and Musculoskeletal Oncology. It will be clinical workshop with emphasis on clinical evaluation of each patient before performing the procedure to confirm the clinically relevant pathology as the pain generator. The workshop will be a hybrid event with no didactic lectures and only hands on sessions on cadavers and live demo of procedures performed by expert faculty on live human subjects with various musculoskeletal pathologies. Also a special session on orthobiologics preparation like Platelet rich plasma/Growth factor concentrate and Bone marrow aspirate concentrate will be part of the workshop.

Why should I attend this conference?
If you are a beginner or non starter or even at intermediate to advanced level in Musculoskeletal Ultrasound, MSK MRI and MSK interventions. This course is just the right platform for you to gain practical skills and knowledge and start your journey in Musculoskeletal Radiology and if you are already at intermediate to advanced level, there are many extremely practical lectures and workshop skills which will further shorten your learning curve towards expert level.

For unforeseen reasons that the event is cancelled. Delegates will get a full refund less bank charges.
