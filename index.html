WHY SHOULD ONE ATTEND
MSS 2024, Jaipur
it offers never before offered in depth hands on workshops in clinical musculoskeletal Radiology , enabling a radiologist to develop clinical skills and enhance their clinical practice in Musculoskeletal Radiology . It’ll be a comprehensive 3 day event covering every practical , theoretical and day to day difficulties faced by a Radiologist in their journey of Musculoskeletal Radiology. Whether you are a resident or a senior consultant, there will be too much to take back from this event for all attendees
Clinical Musculoskeletal examination workshop for a Radiologist 
If you want to ace your MSK Radiology practise, learning clinical skills is an absolute must, this is a practical workshop aimed from beginners to advanced radiologists teaching how to clinically interact and examine a patient with musculoskeletal disorders , teaching you the nuances of clinical MSK Radiology practise.
Arthritis
workshop
Covering in depth details about all forms of arthritis including peripheral inflammatory arthritis and Axial spondyloarthropathies . It’ll be a case based interactive workshop.
Musculoskeletal Ultrasound workshop
Hands on musculoskeletal Ultrasound workshop aimed at Radiologists at beginners and intermediate level skills in Msk Ultrasound , 7:1 delegates - Faculty Ratio
MSK Interventions Hands on workshop on Fresh frozen cadavers - 9:1 Delegates : Cadaver Ratio
Limited seats only Hands on workshop will cover both Ultraound guided and C arm guided MSK Interventions in sports medicine, pain management and Tumors.

Frequently Asked Questions
Quick answers to questions you may have. Can't find what you're looking for? Check out our full documentation.
Why should I attend this conference?
If you are a beginner or non starter or even at intermediate to advanced level in Musculoskeletal Ultrasound, MSK MRI and MSK interventions. This course is just the right platform for you to gain practical skills and knowledge and start your journey in Musculoskeletal Radiology and if you are already at intermediate to advanced level, there are many extremely practical lectures and workshop skills which will further shorten your learning curve towards expert level.
What is the cancellation policy?
The fee is partially refundable and 70 % of the course fee will be refunded if cancellation done upto 60 days before the event by sending an email to mss2024.jaipur@gmail.com till 15th june 2024. Cancellations done after 15th June 2024 are non refundable.
For unforeseen reasons that the event is cancelled. Delegates will get a full refund less bank charges.
What is the location of the Conference Venue?
Rajasthan international centre, Jaipur, Rajasthan
Travel to Jaipur?
Jaipur is extremely well connected to all metro cities , Tier II and Tier III cities in India and to many countries with international flights
How to register for the Conference ?
Just click on register link at the top . Fill the basic details in the form , scan the QR code to pay the registration fee. Attach the payment receipt and submit the form. The registration fee paid will be in Indian rupees. Early Bird registration has been started now. You will receive the payment confirmation mail in some time after verification of payment by MSS Team.
