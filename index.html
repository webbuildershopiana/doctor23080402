WELCOME 
MESSAGE
Dear Friends/Colleagues
On behalf of the organizing team, it is our sheer pleasure to welcome you to International Musculoskeletal Summit(IMS) to be held in Mauritius, the sun-kissed Island in the Indian Ocean. As Mark Twain famously said, ”Mauritius was made first, then Heaven”. The course is aimed at beginners while also covering intermediate level and some advanced topics in Musculoskeletal Radiology/Medicine. The delegates will be welcomed with a meet and greet event on Friday evening and beach side gala dinner accompanied with cultural events on Saturday night. The conference Venue is Long beach Hotel, a luxury 5 star beach resort in eastern part of MauritiusOur carefully curated course has been designed with both novices and seasoned practitioners in mind, encompassing a comprehensive spectrum of Musculoskeletal Radiology/Medicine topics. Whether you're just embarking on your journey or seeking to delve into more advanced discussions, IMS offers something for everyone. To set the tone for this remarkable gathering, we have planned a festive meet and greet event on the enchanting Friday evening, allowing delegates to forge connections and share excitement. And what better way to revel in the unique culture of Mauritius than through a beachside gala dinner, complete with captivating cultural performances, on the vibrant Saturday night.
