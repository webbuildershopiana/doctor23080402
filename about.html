Dear Friends/Colleagues
On behalf of the organizing team, it is our sheer pleasure to welcome you to XIIth Annual conference of Musculoskeletal Society of India to be held in Jaipur , World heritage site as declared by UNESCO. The conference and the 4 hands on workshops are meant for beginners , intermediate as well as advanced level of radiologists in their Musculoskeletal practice with specially curated content for all. The conference Venue is a newly built truly International convention center - Rajasthan International center (RIC). We invite all radiologists from across the globe to come and step into the Royal world of Jaipur , be mesmerized by its beauty and get thrilled by supreme academic content of the conference. DONT MISS OUT!!
