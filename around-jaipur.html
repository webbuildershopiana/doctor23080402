Around jaipur
Pushkar
A sacred town known for the Pushkar Lake and the Brahma Temple. Explore vibrant markets and witness the tranquility of this pilgrimage site.
Ranthambore National Park
A wildlife sanctuary famed for its tiger population. Embark on thrilling safaris to witness diverse flora and fauna in their natural habitat.
Ajmer
Home to the revered Ajmer Sharif Dargah, this city boasts a rich cultural heritage. Explore the Ana Sagar Lake and Taragarh Fort for historical charm.
Shekhawati
A region renowned for its beautifully painted havelis, showcasing intricate frescoes and architectural marvels. A paradise for art and history enthusiasts.
Bhangarh Fort
Often regarded as one of the most haunted places in India, Bhangarh Fort's ruins offer an eerie yet captivating atmosphere amidst the Aravalli Range.
Sariska National Park
A haven for wildlife enthusiasts, Sariska is known for its tiger reserve, diverse fauna, and the historic Kankwari Fort.
Neemrana
Home to the Neemrana Fort Palace, a heritage hotel with a stunning blend of history and luxury. Explore the fort, zip line, and savor a regal experience.
Alwar
Rich in history and architecture, Alwar offers attractions like the City Palace, Siliserh Lake, and the haunted Bhangarh Fort, creating a diverse travel experience.
