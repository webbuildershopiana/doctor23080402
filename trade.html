Message
Dear Trade Partners,
It gives me immense pleasure to send out this invitation to you to take part in this truly international upcoming annual conference of Musculoskeletal Society to be held in the beautiful city of Jaipur from 16-18th August 2024. This workshop styled international conference is truly unique in so many ways with already confirmed participation of more than 20 International faculty with all of them being big names in the world of Musculoskeletal Radiology. Below you will find list of few things which make this event a truly unique opportunity for trade partners looking to venture into or consolidate their market in Musculoskeletal Radiology including Musculoskeletal Ultrasound, Musculoskeletal MRI and Image guided (Ultrasound and C-Arm) Musculoskeletal Interventions:
1. Participation of 500-700 delegates seriously interested in Musculoskeletal Radiology from all parts of India and SAARC countries and many other countries with audience ranging from beginners to advanced users.
2. Refresher course on 17th and 18th August 2024 for residents and beginners in Musculoskeletal Radiology. Our diamond and platinum sponsors will get 10 minutes session each to interact with all the beginner delegates during the sessions.
3. Separate hall for advanced delegates in Musculoskeletal Radiology wherein only advanced speakers, topics will be covered for radiologists already at intermediate to advanced levels of their practise in Musculoskeletal Radiology. The platinum and diamond sponsors will again get 10 minutes each to interact directly with the biggest names in field of Musculoskeletal Radiology in country and around the world during advance sessions.
4. Sports meet of all faculty and society members on 15th August 2024 including cricket match amongst others.
We firmly believe that a scientific event is an opportunity for all delegates to learn about the latest products and technologies in their field which will help them enhance their practise in Musculoskeletal Radiology and this year MSS 2024 serves as a fantastic opportunity for all the trade partners to showcase their products to never been seen numbers and quality of delegates in a exclusive musculoskeletal Radiology event.
